## Summary

A sanitized project page for PHP/Laravel/MySQL-based manufacturing and business-system experience. The focus is manufacturing production-management domain work, maintenance, data handling, on-premise constraints, and gradual legacy improvement.

## Problem

Manufacturing business systems must reflect operational workflows accurately while remaining stable under maintenance and customer-specific change requests. Public portfolio material must describe the engineering signal without exposing factory operations, customer identifiers, internal tickets, screenshots, logs, or metrics.

## Role-Safe Framing

Use these verbs by default until role scope is confirmed:

- participated in maintenance and functional improvement
- handled backend data-processing requests
- worked with legacy PHP/Laravel/MySQL business-system code
- improved readability and module boundaries without publishing private implementation details

Avoid unsupported strong claims such as owned, led, or achieved percentage improvements unless private evidence and role scope are reviewed.
